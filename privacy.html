COOKIES POLICY

"The policy of processing of cookies" (hereinafter – the Policy) – the document defining the order of use of cookies – fragments of the User data sent by the server and stored by Administration to authenticate, to save the settings of the User, to collect statistics about Users in the "Eattable" mobile application (hereinafter – Service).

  • 1. The policy is the integral part of privacy policy of Service which, in turn, – is the integral part of the User agreement of Service (hereinafter – Agreement). Adopting the Agreement, privacy policy, the User unconditionally agrees to use its cookies by Administration.

  • 2. Depending on the settings of the User, the Administration collects information that is automatically transferred when the User visits Service. This transfer is done by cookies. This information, as a rule, includes the IP address now assigned to the User's device, type of the operating system and the used device.

  • 3. Cookie is the small text file which the Web server places in memory of the User`s device. Distinguish between "session" and "persistent" cookies.

  • 4. Administration uses session cookies to assign a unique identification number to the User's device each time he visits Service. Session cookies are used to maintain the functionality of Service and to analyze the User`s work, for example, what pages are visited by the User, what references he uses and how long he stays on each page of Service.

  • 5. Whenever reasonable the Administration uses persistent cookies which are not removed right after Service closes, but saved on the computer during the certain period of time or until the User deletes them himself. Every time as the User visits Service, the Web server will recognize the constant cookies which are stored in memory of the User`s device. Assigning to the User's device the unique identifier, the database of actions and preferences of the User is created. This unique identifier also allows to define:

    • how often the User visits Service,

    • how often he returns to Service,

    • how he uses Service,

    • how effective Service promotion measures are.

  • 6. Cookies do not contain Personal data, and only record actions on the User's device. If the User goes to Service at the link which was sent to its e-mail or if the User creates "user ID" during one of visits, information provided by Service cookies or by third-party service providers cookies can be connected with information in records which will enable User identification.

  • 7. Session cookie-files do not require prior consent of the User to their use as they are necessary for work of Service and will be deleted after Service is closed.

  • 8. Persistent cookies that monitor last actions and preferences of the User, but are not necessary for work of Service, require prior consent of the User. Visiting Service, the User agrees to use permanent cookies if does not change setting of the device to prevent these cookies from being saved. The same applies to the third parties cookies.

  • 9. Cookies are also divided into categories:

    • Category 1 – cookies strictly necessary. These cookies are necessary to view the Service pages and use their functions. Without them, it is impossible to provide services such as shopping cart and online payment.

    • Category 2 – performance cookies. These cookies collect information on the most often visited pages. Such data can be used to optimize websites and simplify navigation. These cookie files are not used to collect personal information. All information collected with their help is intended for statistical purposes and remains anonymous.

    • Category 3 – functional cookies. These cookies allow to remember the choice made by the User when browsing Service. For example, the cookie file can remember the User`s location that will allow to display Service in the language of the User`s country. These files can be also used for storing of such settings as the size and the font of the text as well as other parameters of Service. These files can be also used to track the recommended products and materials in order to avoid repetition. Information provided by such cookies does not allow User identification.

  • 10. The main purpose of using cookies is the construction of Service more User-friendly. To make the User use Service without delays, the Administration at repeated (and each subsequent) use of Service by the User uses User`s data about authentication results when logged into the secure sections of Service, information on the visited pages of Service, preferences and settings of the User and other similar information. The Administration does not collect, store, process or transfer cookies of the User to the third parties, except for statistical purposes of search engines.

  • 11. If the User decides to refuse cookies, he will not be able to use some opportunities of Service.

  • 12. Setup of the device of the User – the free and effective way of managing cookies.

    The User can accept one of the following solutions:

    • allow all cookies integrated into pages to be used. Note: on the one hand, access to these cookie-files will be got only by their publishers; on the other hand, this process is not irreversible, and later the User can always delete these cookie-files (the procedure of managing cookie-files differs depending on the used device);

    • block cookies on the User's device. In that case the User is warned that navigation on Service can be complicated. Some functions require the use of cookies (for example, for recognition of the operating system and the User`s preferred language). Therefore, the Administration is not responsible for the inconveniences caused by inadequate work of services of Service because of disabled cookie-files;

    • enable the use of cookies on a case-by-case basis;

    • accept or reject cookies depending on their publisher.
