Points from EATTABLE Coins can be earned by making a booking in our application.
What are EATTABLE Dining Coins?

You earn EATTABLE Dining Coins when you log into your EATTABLE account and place an order. When you dine, points are credited to your EATTABLE account and can be redeemed for various rewards. Earn X to X points for every booking you make. Click here (link) to register. Note. Takeaway orders do not earn points.

How do I know how many EATTABLE points I've earned?

The conditions for which EATTABLE points are awarded will be displayed during the booking process.

Arriving at the restaurant at the time specified in the reservation.

On the reservation page, switch the slider to get points.

The number of EATTABLE points you can earn depends on:

  • Restaurant

  • Booking dates

  • Booking times

Please note that EATTABLE Points are not eligible for certain types of bookings such as:

  • Bookings for which you have not opted in to receive points on the Booking Summary page.

  • Bookings not made on EATTABLE.com.

  • Bookings made outside of our websites or applications.

  • Bookings made through Dining Coins.

  • Bookings made while you are not logged into your account.

  • Bookings where we indicate otherwise (such as at the time of booking) will not earn points.

  • For more information about eligibility for points and what it takes to earn points, please see these instructions.

Where can I check my current points balance?

You can view the total number of points in the "Wallet" section of the EatTable application.

Do the EATTABLE points expire?

Eatable coins are permanent.
